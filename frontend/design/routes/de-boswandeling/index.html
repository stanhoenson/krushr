Creating a route

To create a route you need to fill out the form(s) below. Some fields have a black button to add a new entity of said field. When you press this button, a new empty field will be displayed.

Adding points of interest

A route also consists of points of interest, and this form looks very similar to the route form. The biggest difference are latitude and longitude fields (and the big map). You can type these out manually or click a spot on the map.

Just like with the other fields, press the black button beneath the point of interest form, to add a new one.
